About Us

Where Luxury Meets the Wild.

At Klein Safaris, we redefine luxury travel with tailor-made journeys through the heart of Kenya. Our curated safaris combine elegance, comfort, and adventure, taking you from the iconic Maasai Mara to the majestic Mount Kenya and the serene Indian Ocean coast. Each itinerary is crafted with precision, offering intimate encounters with wildlife, breathtaking landscapes, and rich cultural experiences. With expert private guides and handpicked accommodations, we ensure every moment of your journey is seamless and unforgettable. Beyond exploration, we are dedicated to conservation and community partnerships, allowing your travel to create a lasting impact. Klein Safaris is more than a safari company—we are your gateway to Africa’s most exclusive and timeless adventures
